We are delighted to announce our participation in BioCHIP Berlin 2026, the 6th International Forum on Biochips and Microfabrication, happening from May 19–20, in Berlin, Germany.

The BioCHIP Berlin aims to showcase the latest fabrication methodologies for microfluidic systems and related microsystems, as well as emerging applications. This sector-focused event brings together scientists, engineers, manufacturers, and end users to foster interdisciplinary dialogue, driving advancements across the microfluidics technology value chain and enhancing translational applications.

At this year’s exhibition, we are showcasing our latest developments in plasma technology for microfluidics and microsystems. Visitors will have the opportunity to speak directly with our team of experts to explore tailored applications in:

  • Plasma systems specifically design for microfluidic manufacturing.
  • Functionalised glass slides with chemistries such as epoxide, amine, carboxylic acid, etc.
  • Tailored smart substrates: hydrophobic/hydrophilic, cell-adhesive/cell-repellent, charge-engineered.

We warmly invite you to visit our booth and learn more about how our innovations can support your business and R&D needs. Whether you are seeking new solutions or technical insights, our team looks forward to welcoming you in Berlin.
